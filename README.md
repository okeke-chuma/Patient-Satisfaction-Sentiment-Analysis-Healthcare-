Patient-Satisfaction-Sentiment-Analysis-Healthcare-

Analyze patient comments to identify positive, negative, and neutral experiences.
Can also be used for other industries/entities like Pharma, Lifesciences, Biomedical Sciences, Insurance
Typically patient/customer survey files are obtained from publicly avilable sources.
  For Healthcare: Data sources could be  
      Hospital patient reviews
      CMS Hospital Reviews
      Kaggle Patient Reviews
      Leapfrog Reviews
      Patient Experiences & Quality: The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality
      National Statistics: The CDC Public-Use NCHS Data
      Open HHS Data: The HealthData.gov 
      Global Health Insights: The Data at WHO
      Broader Search: The Google Dataset Search
      Definitive Healthcare (Healthcare Demographics Data - Real-World Patient Data)
